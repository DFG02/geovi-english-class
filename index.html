Future Expressions
§1
ExpressionUse / Uso
Going toPlanes decididos · I'm going to travel.
GonnaInformal "going to" · I'm gonna call you.
WillDecisión espontánea / promesa · I'll help you!
Won'tNegación con will · I won't be late.
WouldDeseo / condicional · I would love to go.
MightPosibilidad · I might be late.
MayPosibilidad formal · She may call later.
About toFuturo inmediato · I'm about to eat.
Present ContinuousPlanes fijos/citas · I'm meeting her at 9.
Simple PresentHorarios / itinerarios · The bus leaves at 8.
Be supposed toSe espera que / deber · You're supposed to be there.
Be planning toEstar planeando · I'm planning to visit.
Quick Examples
  • I'm going to study. / I'm gonna study.
  • I will call you later. (decisión ahora mismo)
  • I might be late — not sure yet.
  • I'm about to leave, wait!
  • The train leaves at half past eight.
